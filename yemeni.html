YEMENI DAY


Yemeni unification is the merging of North Yemen and South Yemen into one state, the "Republic of Yemen," declared on May 22, 1990. The agreement was signed by Ali Abdullah Saleh from North Yemen and Ali Salem al-Beidh from South Yemen. The unification established a joint government and parliament with a focus on democracy and political pluralism.Today, the goal is to achieve stability and sustainable development in a unified Yemen, emphasizing cooperation and national unity to build a better future for the country.
